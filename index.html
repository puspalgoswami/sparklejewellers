The Workshop

Made by hand, checked twice

Behind every case at Sparkle is a bench, a loupe, and a craftsman who has usually been at this longer than the shop has had a signboard. Nothing leaves the workshop until it's been checked by hand — for weight, for finish, for the small details that separate jewellery from decoration.
